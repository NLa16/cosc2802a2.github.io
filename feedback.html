Feedback

Coming up with this idea, I know that I will need to receive feedback from many people in order to find problems involved in my project. So I have asked for my teammates’ opinions on my project. And they all have different thoughts on it.

One suggests that I can add a journal for artists. This feature will help them update their followers on what has been happening in their art progress, or simply, what has been happening in that artist’s life. So it will work like a little “diary”. This is an interesting idea. However, the problem is this is actually a feature already available in DeviantArt, so maybe we can twist it a little to make it look more original and to avoid copyright issues?

Another suggestion is that I should have a page to provide tutorials for those who are new to art. They are going to be tutorials posted by experienced artists, and this will help newbie artists in starting out and improving their art skills. This is not a new idea, but I think this is absolutely necessary. Even though no one has an identical art skill with someone else, tutorials are important in order to find one’s unique art style.
