Team Profile

Team Name

I have carefully taken into consideration about team name before choosing one for the whole team. I have come into an understanding that a team name has to reflect a workgroup where every individual in the team is different and each of our ideas will be as well. So I have chosen a name that will most likely describe a team full of unique personalities: The Colors. “The Colors” has a very special meaning to it. Just like colors, it consists of members whose ideas are very original and creative. But not only that, it also shows that each person will have their own unique talent, and will be able to contribute differently to current and future team projects.

Team Profile

I was able to find myself a team of amazing people with unique personalities and talents. It seems that my personality fits with them as well. To my surprise, there are a total of three Turbulent Mediators (including me), and there is a Debater as well. That means that there are people who think just like me, and working with other Mediators will strengthen what this special personality has for me: thoughtful, open-minded, creative, and passionate. Working with a Debater will gain the team a big advantage, because they are quick thinkers who are very flexible with ideas and are very knowledgeable with new concepts, which will help each member develop with their own ideas.
